CyberSpark Quest Privacy Policy

CyberSpark Quest

Effective Date: July 8, 2026

Introduction

CyberSpark Quest ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our educational game.

CyberSpark Quest is an educational game designed for students and beginners to develop logical thinking, mathematics, computer hardware knowledge, cybersecurity awareness, debugging concepts, and problem-solving skills through interactive gameplay.

By using CyberSpark Quest, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

CyberSpark Quest does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as:

Name
Email address
Phone number
Home address
Contacts
Photos
Videos

We do not operate our own servers to store personal user information.

Game Progress

The app stores gameplay information locally on your device, including:

Completed levels
Coins earned
Selected avatar
Game progress and achievements
App preferences

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Advertising

Google AdMob may collect and process certain information, such as device identifiers and app interaction data, to provide, improve, and measure advertisements. This information is collected and processed by Google in accordance with its own Privacy Policy.
