# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 3, 2026

Welcome to **Magnet Master**. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Magnet Master collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the game.

Magnet Master does not require users to create an account.

Depending on the features you use, the app may collect limited information, including:

* Device information (device model, operating system version)
* App performance and crash reports
* Gameplay progress stored on your device
* Advertising identifiers (when applicable)
* Anonymous analytics to improve gameplay

We do not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, phone number, or email address through the game.

## 2. Advertising

Magnet Master may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners such as Google AdMob.

These advertising providers may collect certain device information or advertising identifiers to provide and measure ads. Their data collection is governed by their own privacy policies.

## 3. In-App Purchases

If the game offers in-app purchases, all purchases are securely processed through the app store (such as Google Play). We do not receive or store your payment information.

## 4. Analytics

We may use analytics services to understand how players use the game, including:

* Levels completed
* Session duration
* Game performance
* Crash reports

This information is used only to improve the game experience.

## 5. Local Storage

Magnet Master stores game progress locally on your device, including:

* Unlocked levels
* Coins
* Gems
* Achievements
* Selected skins
* Game settings

This data is used only to provide game functionality.

Magnet Master is intended for a general audience.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the game, please contact us so we can address the issue.

Parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise children's use of mobile applications.
